## Cold Chain Monitoring & IoT

Sensors, tracking systems, and IoT platforms for monitoring temperature and environmental conditions.

- [Controlant](https://www.controlant.com/) — IoT-based temperature monitoring and supply chain visibility platform.
- [Monnit](https://www.monnit.com/) — Wireless sensors for monitoring temperature, humidity, and environmental conditions.
- [Sensitech](https://www.sensitech.com/) — Cold chain monitoring solutions with real-time tracking and analytics.
- [Tive](https://www.tive.com/) — Real-time shipment tracking with temperature and location monitoring.

## Logistics & Supply Chain Platforms

Platforms for managing cold chain logistics, transportation, and distribution.

- [Descartes Systems Group](https://www.descartes.com/) — Logistics and supply chain management platform with cold chain capabilities.
- [FourKites](https://www.fourkites.com/) — Real-time supply chain visibility platform.
- [Project44](https://www.project44.com/) — End-to-end supply chain visibility and tracking platform.
- [SAP Logistics Business Network](https://www.sap.com/products/business-network/logistics.html) — Digital platform for logistics collaboration and management.

## Temperature-Controlled Packaging

Solutions for maintaining temperature stability during storage and transport.

- [Cold Chain Technologies](https://www.coldchaintech.com/) — Temperature-controlled packaging solutions for pharmaceuticals and biologics.
- [Pelican BioThermal](https://www.pelicanbiothermal.com/) — Reusable and single-use thermal packaging solutions.
- [Sonoco ThermoSafe](https://www.sonocothermosafe.com/) — Insulated packaging solutions for temperature-sensitive shipments.

## Pharmaceutical Cold Chain

Platforms and technologies specifically designed for transporting vaccines, biologics, and medical products.

- [Envirotainer](https://www.envirotainer.com/) — Active temperature-controlled air cargo containers for pharmaceuticals.
- [SkyCell](https://skycells.ch/) — Hybrid containers with IoT monitoring for pharmaceutical logistics.
- [Va-Q-Tec](https://va-q-tec.com/) — Thermal containers and passive cold chain solutions.

## Food & Agriculture Cold Chain

Systems and platforms supporting temperature-controlled storage and distribution of food products.

- [Carrier Transicold](https://www.carrier.com/truck-trailer/en/worldwide/) — Refrigeration solutions for transport and cold storage.
- [Lineage Logistics](https://www.lineagelogistics.com/) — Temperature-controlled warehousing and logistics provider.
- [Thermo King](https://www.thermoking.com/) — Refrigerated transport solutions for food and perishable goods.

## Standards & Compliance

Regulations, standards, and frameworks governing cold chain logistics and safety.

- [FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)](https://www.fda.gov/food/food-safety-modernization-act-fsma) — U.S. regulation for food safety across supply chains.
- [GDP (Good Distribution Practice)](https://www.ema.europa.eu/en/human-regulatory/overview/public-health-threats/good-distribution-practice) — Guidelines for pharmaceutical distribution quality.
- [WHO Cold Chain Guidelines](https://www.who.int/teams/immunization-vaccines-and-biologicals/supply-chain) — Guidance for vaccine storage and transport.
